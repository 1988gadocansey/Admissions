Frequently asked questions

What is the address for the TTU admission portal?

Logon to admissions.ttuportal.com

When can I check my admission status as a graduate student?

This is determined by the department running the programme. You'll receive SMS to confirm your admission. Frequently, you'll receive receive email alert from us. You can keep visiting the portal for weekly updates

What do I do when I get to know I have been admitted?

1. Login to the admission portal to print your admission letter.
2. Pay your fees are the banks listed on your letter or pay on your admission portal.
3. After payment logon to the admission portal to book your accomodation.
4. Arrive on campus on the date started on the letter.

Feel free to contact us and we'll help you out as soon as we can.

After filling the online form, what next?

Online print-out should be added to the admission forms filled manually and posted through DHL (For International Students )or EMS Delivery Services (for local students).

Where can I pay my admission fees?

Logon to the apply.ttuportal.com and click on the finance menu(side menu). Click on view fees. You can proceed further to make the payment online by clicking pay fees or the listed banks (CBG, Ecobank, Prudential and the rest.)

How do I book accommodation(selection of rooms and bed no.)?

a. Logon to your portal, admissions.ttuportal.com and click the book accommodation icon.
b. Click on Accomodation menu and select the room and bed respectively.
c. Follow the sms alert on your phone to pay the hall fees. d. You have maximum of two days to make payment else you will loose your room.

Course Registration

a. After successfully paying your fees, you will receive your institional email and index number with password to access the student portal.
b. Logon to records.ttuportal.com to register your courses and get your teaching and exams timetables.

Orientation Programme?

Orientation starts a week after the arrival. All students are required to attend.
